- **Accomplishments:**
- Learned three different methods for making 白切鸡 (White Cut Chicken):
  - **Traditional Cantonese method**: Submerge whole chicken in boiling water, turn off heat, let sit 30 minutes, then ice bath for crispy skin
  - **Rice cooker method**: Place chicken in rice cooker with ginger and scallions, cook until done (simpler, higher success rate)

- Identified rice cooker method as most beginner-friendly with highest success rate
- Key technical insight: Ice bath step is essential for achieving crispy skin in traditional method
